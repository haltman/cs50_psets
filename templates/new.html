How to Play:

Welcome to Flow! Get ready for some estrogen-infused fun.

When you're ready, select the 'Start' button below.

You will begin in Round 1: She said what?, where you will have to match famous quotes with their authors.

If you match 3 out of the 5 quotes correctly, you will advance to Round 2: Who's on first?

There you must select whether each 'first' for women in American history occurred before or after the year displayed.

Choose correctly at least 3 times in the 28 seconds alloted and move on to the last and final Round 3: Name that woman!

Read a prompt from each of the 4 categories (Pop Culture, Politics, Arts, Sports) and select the woman it's describing.

Choose 3 or more women correctly and you win!
